Smart Health Monitoring System

Wearable device to monitor Pulse rate and temperature of cab drivers

The main objective of the project is to monitor & analyse a cab driver’s pulse rate, body temperature & haemoglobin levels and inform the end user if the cab driver’s health condition is good or bad.

A wearable device was designed and made to monitor the pulse rate, body temperature & haemoglobin, analyse the sensor data and then inform the user about the cab drivers health status using Bluetooth module and an app designed for this purpose.

The data can be interfaced with ride booking / sharing applications where healthy car driver will be allotted to the end user for safe ride.

  • Guide: Dr. Naveen Kumar
  • Students: Vyshak Nath C A, M Abhay Vardhan

    • Agrimax Smart Urban Hydroponics

    Soil-less Smart Agriculture System

    An Intelligent Hydroponics System that lets Urban Agri Consumers become potential crop Prosumers (Buyer + Seller).

    The project is focused upon crop cultivation of common vegetables without soil. An Android application updates the pH, DO, turbidity values of the water in real-time.

    The nutrients are supplied to the system for plant growth and continuous water flow is maintained.Currently, the focus is upon automation process of the complete system without or very little human involvement.

    • Stair Climbing Wheel Chair

    Less Jerk Stair Climbing Wheel Chair

    The primary objective of this project is to build a low cost less jerk Stair Climbing Wheel Chair.

    A 5 Axis smooth and stable mechanism to climb stairs as well as move on a plane surface.

    The wheel chair will be of great help for senior citizens in climbing the staircases up and down with minimal effort.

    Automation of the prototype is under consideration. Currently, a three wheeled conveyer belt based design is being developed and soon prototype will be developed.

    • Ecomile

    Electric Cycle

    The primary objective of this project is to build a self powering machine.

    This cycle basically runs on 2 modes: The motoring mode, in which it acts as an electric cycle & the generation mode in which the cycle acts as a normal cycle but the mechanical energy is converted into electrical energy.

    The system is under further development to bring out a full fledged system capable of generating electricity with higher efficiency.

    The design of the E-Bike is under further development to increase the efficiency of the battery and speed.

    • Portable Fingerprint Attendance system

    Smart Attendance for Classrooms

    The project is aimed at designing Fingerprint Recognition System based portable attendance system which will manage the student attendance at IIITDM.

    The project is based on Raspberry Pi which is used to recognise and mark the attendance of the saved fingerprints automatically.

    This reduces the time taken in manual attendance system and gives great accuracy.

    The system is integrated to web server where the attendance is updated automatically and is reflected for users.

    • Thermo Acoustic Engine

    Conversion of Waste Heat to Thermo Acoustics

    The main objective of this Thermo Acoustic Engine to convert Heat to Acoustic Wave and ultimately into electricity or mechanical work by the means of using Linear Alternator.

    The four main parts of the Engine are: Regenerator, Hot-Heat Exchanger, Cold-Heat Exchanger & Resonator Tube.

    The prototype converts low grade of energy(heat) into high grade of energy(work), provides green and renewable energy which is pollution and carbon free and thus minimize the wastage of heat energy.

  • Guide: Dr. Naveen Kumar
  • Students: Subodh Kumar


    • Solar/Thermal Stirling Engine

    Renewable Energy Application

    A solar/ thermal stirling engine developed at DIC IIITDM Kancheepuram.

    The engine can be employed in any environment where there is a temperature difference maintained. One end of the cylinder will be placed on the hotter side and the other end at a lower temperature.

    Since the depletion of fossil fuels will be an issue in the foreseeable future, the Stirling engine is an incredible invention which is too important to be overlooked in the necessary quest for alternate energy resources.
